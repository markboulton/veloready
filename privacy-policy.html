Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2025

VeloReady ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the VeloReady mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Health Data (via Apple HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, VeloReady accesses the following health data from Apple Health:

  • Sleep data: Sleep duration, sleep stages, and sleep quality metrics
  • Heart rate variability (HRV): Used for recovery score calculation
  • Resting heart rate (RHR): Used for recovery and health monitoring
  • Respiratory rate: Used for wellness tracking
  • Heart rate: During activities and at rest
  • Active calories: Energy expenditure during activities
  • Step count: Daily activity tracking

Activity Data (via Third-Party Services)

When you connect Strava or Intervals.icu, we access:

  • Cycling activities: Rides, workouts, and training sessions
  • Power data: Watts, normalized power, intensity factor
  • Heart rate zones: Training zone distribution
  • Training metrics: TSS, IF, NP, and other performance indicators
  • FTP data: Functional Threshold Power settings

App Usage Data

  • Settings and preferences: Your app configuration choices
  • OAuth tokens: Securely stored authentication tokens for connected services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide and improve VeloReady's features:

  • Calculate scores: Recovery, sleep quality, and training load scores
  • Analyze activities: Provide insights on your cycling performance
  • Track trends: Show your fitness progression over time
  • Personalize experience: Adapt recommendations to your training
  • Sync data: Keep your data updated from connected services

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your health data or activity data to any VeloReady servers.

Security Measures

  • HealthKit encryption: Health data is encrypted by iOS
  • Keychain storage: OAuth tokens stored securely in iOS Keychain
  • No cloud backup: Your sensitive data is not backed up to our servers
  • Secure connections: All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, certain app data (settings, preferences, and Core Data entities) may be synced to your personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted and only accessible to you through your Apple ID.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

When you connect third-party services, data is shared only as follows:

  • Strava: We access your activities and profile data via Strava's API. Data is governed by Strava's privacy policy.
  • Intervals.icu: We access your training data via their API. Data is governed by Intervals.icu's privacy policy.

You can disconnect these services at any time from the app's Settings.

No Analytics or Tracking

VeloReady does not use:

  • Third-party analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Advertising networks or trackers
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • HealthKit permissions: Manage in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health
  • OAuth connections: Disconnect services in VeloReady Settings
  • Delete data: Remove all app data via Settings → Reset All Data
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data

Data Portability

Your data remains in Apple Health and your connected services. You can export data from these services directly.

6. Children's Privacy

VeloReady is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of VeloReady after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Consent: You explicitly grant permission to access HealthKit data
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide the app's services
  • Legitimate interest: Improving app functionality and user experience

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as:

  • You have the app installed on your device
  • You maintain connections to third-party services
  • You choose to keep your data in the app

You can delete all data at any time through the app's Settings.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored locally on your device. When you connect third-party services (Strava, Intervals.icu), data may be transferred to servers operated by those services in various countries. Please review their privacy policies for details.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Note: VeloReady does not sell personal information.
